About

I got into computers at a young age. I thought they were really interesting and liked the challenges they presented. I fell into programming while I was trying to become a writer in college. When I’m not behind a keyboard I read, make music or take pictures. I also do some cooking here and there.

I am currently a Android developer for Starcor. Before that I worked at REG as a Construction Management Intern.

I have a strict father and mother (but I was a really naughty boy when I was young...), and I have two sisters, I love them all.

Like a growing number of my peers I find digital things convenient but boring. I enjoy listening to music on live, play games on reality, and reading books on paper. It is something of an irony that I make my living slinging bits. Maybe one day we’ll figure out how to put the Internet onto something analog.
